Live Scoring Flow
  1. One golfer in each foursome opens the live scorecard link and enters hole-by-hole scores.
  2. Every score save updates the shared Tin Cup live data, and the leaderboard reflects changes immediately.
  3. Match Scoring is auto-derived from real scorecards: completed segments show W/T/L, in-progress segments show current leader (W*/L*/T*).
