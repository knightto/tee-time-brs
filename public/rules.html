Weekend Game Rules

Entry Fee: $15 per player
Breakdown:
  • $5 – 3-Putt Poker
  • $5 – Skins Game
  • $5 – Closest to the Pin (all 4 par 3s)

3-Putt Poker

Everyone starts with 2 cards. You gain or lose cards and money based on putting:

  • 3-putt: Add $1 to the poker pot.
  • 1-putt: Gain 1 card.
  • 0-putt (chip-in or holed from off the green): Gain 2 cards.

The Chip:

  • The chip is held by the most recent player to 3-putt in the group.
  • If you are holding the chip and 3-putt again, you owe an extra $1 penalty.

Winner:

  • Best 5-card poker hand at the end of the round wins the poker pot.
  • Standard poker hand rankings apply.

Skins Game

  • Each hole is worth 1 skin.
  • Lowest outright score on a hole wins that skin.
  • Ties carry the skin to the next hole.
  • Total skins pot is split according to number of skins won.

Handicap Strokes (Pops):

  • 12–15 handicap: Gets a pop (stroke) on holes 4, 7, 15, 16
  • 17+ handicap: Gets a pop (stroke) on holes 1, 4, 7, 11, 15, 16

Closest to the Pin (CTP)

  • All 4 par 3s are CTP holes.
  • Closest to the pin on the green wins that hole's CTP share of the pot.

Special CTP rules:

  • If the current CTP player 3-putts, they lose CTP, and it reverts to the previous closest.
  • If two players appear to tie for CTP and no one is clearly closer, they split that hole's CTP.
  • If nobody hits the green, that hole's CTP money is split among the hole's winners (by score).
